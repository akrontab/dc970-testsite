DC970 is a community for security professionals and enthusiasts in northern Colorado. We meet monthly to share knowledge, tools, and research — from defensive blue team work to offensive red team techniques, CTF challenges, and everything in between.

## Meetings

We meet on the third Thursday of each month. Meeting details including location and agenda are posted here a few weeks in advance.

All skill levels are welcome. There are no dues or membership requirements — just show up.

## Contact

Join us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/qxxxUhYbT5) to connect between meetings, share links, and get notified when new meetups are announced.
